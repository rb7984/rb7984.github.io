Riccardo Barelli - Computational Designer

I’ve got an Architectural background having graduated in Architecture and Civil Engineering in Bologna. During the time spent in university I’ve developed a passion for 3D modeling and 3D visualization, using digital tools ranging from AutoCad 2D and 3D to Rhino with Grasshopper, and exploring rendering techniques with Vray or Cinema 4D or 3D Studio Max. This experience helped me a lot during my curricular stage conducted at WASP, exploring the potential of 3D printed housing processes. Here I’ve developed the design for Dior Pop-Up stores that has been exhibited at EXPO21 in Dubai. I also bring with me an additional knowledge in Computational Design developed writing my Master’s degree thesis: I’ve explored the architectural potential that a computational approach could express if we decide to apply it in the design process. I always bring enthusiasm and precision in what I'm doing and I'm not scared to be put in uncomfortable places if it means I'm learning something new.
